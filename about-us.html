BookVerse Mission

Our mission in creating this library website, which is from the Human and Computer Interaction course assignment project. Where the assignment aims to discuss about understanding the context of the problem and the use of interactive systems to be designed. In addition, it also discusses fundamental concepts in human and computer interaction and their application to produce modern and usable user interfaces.
